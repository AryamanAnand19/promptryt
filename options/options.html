Privacy

🔒 What data is processed: Your prompt text, only when you click the PromptRyt button.

🔑 AI Mode: Prompts are sent to Anthropic's API using your personal API key. Anthropic's Privacy Policy applies.

🏠 Rule-Based Mode: All processing happens entirely in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.

📦 Storage: Only your settings and API key are stored locally in Chrome. No external servers.
